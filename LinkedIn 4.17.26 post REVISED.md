## REVISED POST

3 questions every firm should ask their SaaS vendor today:

1. "Where exactly does our data sit right now — and who at your company can access it?"

Most vendors say "encrypted at rest." That's not an answer. Ask for the specific region, the access control list, and whether their own engineers can decrypt it during support tickets. (Most can.)

2. "If we cancel tomorrow, what happens to our data — and how long do you actually keep it?"

Retention policies are buried in ToS. Some vendors keep backups for 90+ days after cancellation. That's 90 days your client files live on infrastructure you no longer control.

3. "Can you show us the last time a subprocessor had access to our environment?"

If they can't answer this in under a day, they don't have real audit controls. They have a compliance checkbox.

These aren't gotcha questions. They're the same ones regulators ask during breach investigations — just asked *before* something goes wrong.

We built AION OS to eliminate these questions entirely — 100% on-premise, no cloud calls, no third-party data access. But even if you never use our product, asking these 3 questions will tell you more about your vendor's security posture than their SOC 2 report will.
