## Attorney Departure Risk Analysis

### Overview
**Critical enterprise feature for law firms**: When attorneys leave, massive vulnerability windows open. AION OS identifies and quantifies these risks BEFORE departure, giving firms 30 days to close gaps.

### The Problem
Every attorney departure creates:
- **Knowledge Loss**: 12+ years of institutional knowledge walks out
- **Active Case Risk**: Cases near trial with incomplete documentation
- **Security Gaps**: System access not properly revoked
- **Client Defection**: Relationships follow attorney to new firm
- **Compliance Issues**: Conflicts of interest, ethical violations
- **Competitive Intelligence**: Insider knowledge goes to competitor

**Industry Reality**: Average cost per partner departure = $500k-$2M in losses

### AION OS Solution
Adversarial analysis that identifies:

**1. Knowledge Transfer Gaps**
- Specialized knowledge not documented
- Undocumented case strategies
- High-complexity cases at risk
- Required: 10+ hours knowledge transfer sessions

**2. Active Case Vulnerabilities**
- Cases with trials within 60 days of departure
- Documentation completeness scores
- Deadline conflicts
- Required: Immediate co-counsel assignment

**3. Security & Access Control**
- All system access requiring revocation
- Data exfiltration risk assessment
- Unusual access pattern monitoring
- Required: Access revocation checklist

**4. Client Relationship Risks**
- High-value clients likely to follow attorney
- Client reassurance strategy
- Non-solicitation agreement review
- Required: Partner-level client outreach

**5. Compliance & Ethical Issues**
- Conflict checks with destination firm
- Ethical wall requirements
- Bar association compliance
- Required: Exit interview documentation

**6. Competitive Intelligence Leakage**
- Strategic information at risk
- Operational knowledge exposure
- Restrictive covenant enforcement
- Required: Expedited departure if high risk

### Output
- **Risk Score**: 0-100 (CRITICAL/HIGH/MODERATE/LOW)
- **Vulnerability Analysis**: P0-P4 ranked issues with exploitation scenarios
- **30-Day Transition Plan**: Day-by-day action items
- **Critical Actions**: Top 10 immediate requirements
