## PHASE 1: FOCUS (Months 1-3)

### Pick ONE Vertical: SECURITY (Not Legal)

**Why Security First:**
- Faster sales cycles (3-6 months vs 12-18 months for law firms)
- Clear ROI: $50k pen test → $5k/month AION
- Less regulatory friction
- Tech buyers adopt AI faster than law firms
- Proven with Test 2 (found 5 vulnerabilities in AWS config)

**Legal becomes Phase 2** after security traction.

### Immediate Actions:

1. **Rebrand focus: "AI Red Team Platform"**
   - Homepage: "Continuous AI-Powered Penetration Testing"
   - Kill "adversarial intelligence" (too abstract)
   - Own "AI Red Team" before competitors

2. **Build 5 design partners**
   - Target: Series B-D startups (have security budget, move fast)
   - Offer: Free 3-month pilot in exchange for testimonials
   - Goal: 5 companies running weekly AION scans by Month 3

3. **Ship enterprise features**
   - SSO/SAML integration
   - Team management
   - Scheduled automated scans
   - Slack/PagerDuty integrations
   - Compliance reports (SOC2, ISO27001)
