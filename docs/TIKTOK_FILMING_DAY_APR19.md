**Hook type:** Pattern interrupt

every M&A deal,

14,000 documents in 72 hours before resigning.

Nobody noticed for 11 months.

And your cloud security tools?
They're sending your data to someone else's servers
just to tell you about it.

So now you've got two problems.

And nobody catches it.
Until we did."
