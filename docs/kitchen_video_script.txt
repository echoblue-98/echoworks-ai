ECHOWORKS — "YOUR KITCHEN" VIDEO SCRIPT
========================================
Duration: 45-50 seconds
Format: LinkedIn native video + Instagram Reel
Concept: Rented chaos vs owned calm (kitchen metaphor for data sovereignty)

SCRIPT
------

[SCENE: Chaotic kitchen footage — noise, crowding, too many hands]

VOICEOVER:
"This is how most law firms run their cybersecurity.
Borrowed tools. Shared infrastructure. Someone else's kitchen."

[Quick cut — even more chaos, plates crashing or near-misses]

VOICEOVER:
"Your client data sits on someone else's servers.
Their cooks touch your recipes.
And when something goes wrong — you're the last to know."

[Hard cut to silence. You, alone in a clean kitchen. Calm. Focused. Cooking.]

VOICEOVER:
"We built something different."

[Slow, deliberate shots of you cooking — controlled, precise movements]

VOICEOVER:
"One kitchen. One chef. No outside hands.
Everything stays on your servers.
Every detection pattern built for how your firm actually works."

[Final shot — you plating the dish, clean and composed]

VOICEOVER:
"Your data. Your kitchen. Nobody else touches it."


PRODUCTION NOTES
----------------
- Chaos footage: Royalty-free stock (Pexels, Pixabay — search "busy restaurant kitchen")
  Zero copyright risk. Same emotional effect as TV clips.
- Solo kitchen scene: Film on phone. Natural light, clean counter, simple dish.
- Audio: No background music during your kitchen scene — silence IS the point.
  Optional low bass hum during chaos clips for contrast.
- Keep total runtime under 60 seconds for LinkedIn algorithm.
- Post as native video (upload directly, don't share a YouTube link).
