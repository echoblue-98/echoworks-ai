PLAINTIFF'S MOTION TO DISMISS

The plaintiff respectfully moves this Court to dismiss the defendant's counterclaim for failure to state a claim upon which relief can be granted.

ARGUMENT

The defendant alleges breach of contract but fails to identify which specific contractual provision was allegedly breached. The complaint contains only conclusory statements without factual support.

Furthermore, the plaintiff complied with all notice requirements under Section 12.4 of the Agreement by sending written notice via certified mail on January 15, 2024.

WHEREFORE, plaintiff respectfully requests that this Court grant the motion to dismiss with prejudice.
